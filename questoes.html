Separamos algumas Questões para você

1 - Complete a frase: She __________ to the gym every morning.

- A) go

- B) goes

- C) is going

2 - Escolha a opção correta: They usually __________ lunch at noon.

- A) have

- B) has

- C) are having

3 - Qual é a forma correta da terceira pessoa do singular do verbo "to study" (estudar) no Simple Present?

- A) studys

- B) studyes

- C) studies

4 - Indique a alternativa correta: I __________ my sister every Sunday.

- A) visit

- B) visits

- C)is visiting

5 - Qual é a forma negativa correta da frase: "She always drinks tea"?

- A) She not always drinks tea.

- B) She don't always drink tea.

- C) She doesn't always drink tea.

6 - Escolha a opção correta para a pergunta: "Do they play tennis on weekends?"

- A) Yes, they do.

- B) Yes, they does.

- C) No, they don't.

7 - Complete a sentença com a forma correta do Simple Present: "The train __________ at 9:00 AM."

- A) leave

- B) leaves

- C) leaving
