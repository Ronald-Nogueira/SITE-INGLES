Teaching Tools

We have also separated 3 tools that can assist in your learning.

Duolingo is a free and fun language learning platform with short lessons, exercises and interactive games.

Cambly is an online language teaching platform that connects students with native teachers through video calls, offering classes focused on improving conversational skills.

ChatGPT can be used as an interactive tool to learn English, offering answers to questions, information and help with language practice through conversations, making learning more dynamic and engaging.
